Be Honest 2017 is the 8th annual Portland State University Graphic Design (PSUGD) student portfolio showcase! It's part portfolio show, part party, part open house, part alumni reunion, part pop-up shop, part panel discussion, and ALL FUN.


Be Honest serves as a valuable opportunity for Portland State University graphic design students to gain experience presenting their work and engaging with the community, and for creative professionals to provide their feedback and support. Come and celebrate with us, and check out all the great things coming out of the PSUGD program!

GOOD Market

5:00–8:00pm, Pop-up Shop

Friends of Graphic Design's pop-up shop featuring one-of-a-kind goods from PSUGD students, is also back at Be Honest with art prints, cards, zines, posters, jewelry, stickers, buttons, and much more available for sale. All proceeds from sales go directly to the student designers!
